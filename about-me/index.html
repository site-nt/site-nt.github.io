About Me

Dr. Nicholaides is a Licensed Clinical Psychologist specializing in prenatal and postpartum issues. She has a bachelor’s from the University of Virginia and a doctorate from Widener University’s Institute for Graduate Clinical Psychology. She did her clinical internship at Swarthmore's Counseling and Psychological Services, and her postdoctoral rotation at the University of Pennsylvania Counseling and Psychological Services.

When you first meet Dr. Nicholaides, you’ll probably notice that she tries her hardest to understand you and where you’re coming from. She also does her best to create a peaceful environment by using warmth, congeniality, and humor (when appropriate). She hopes her clients feel they have a safe and open space to overcome their difficulties, and experience new possibilities in this season of life.
